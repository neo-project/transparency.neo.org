Neo Foundation Temporary Committee Expenditure Disclosure

This page is designed to continuously disclose every use of funds promoted by, or decided with the participation of, the temporary committee, subject to community oversight, and to provide a verifiable and traceable public record for future governance reform.

About this page

Given the extraordinary circumstances currently facing Neo Foundation, this page serves as the temporary committee’s public disclosure portal. It is primarily intended to disclose NF expenditure records, use-of-funds explanations, relevant addresses, and update information.

Information disclosed on this page follows the principles of timeliness, specificity, and verifiability. Any use of funds promoted by, or decided with the participation of, the temporary committee should be disclosed to the community in due course and remain subject to community oversight.

Disclosure Principles

This page should remain as public, clear, and traceable as possible, and may be expanded over time as the governance mechanism evolves.

Timely disclosure

Any use of funds promoted by, or decided with the participation of, the temporary committee should be disclosed in due course.

Clear purpose

Each expenditure should, where possible, explain its purpose, amount, asset type, and relevant context.

On-chain verifiability

Where on-chain transactions are involved, the address or transaction details should also be disclosed to enable community verification.

Subject to oversight

If the community has questions about fund usage, it should be able to raise inquiries and conduct review based on the public records.

Records can be filtered by date, status, asset type, or keyword.

Update Policy

Record-by-record disclosure

Each expenditure should be recorded separately, including its purpose, amount, asset, date, status, and any necessary notes.

Governance transition

Once a new governance mechanism is established, this page can transition smoothly into a long-term financial transparency portal.
